Welcome to Stop The Truckery

Stop The Truckery is a competitive PvP vehicle combat game where players battle inside contained arenas using powerful and unique vehicles. Crash into opponents to earn coins, unlock new cars, collect badges, and climb the leaderboard. Every vehicle has different strengths, allowing players to choose their own playstyle and strategy. Stay in your vehicle, survive the chaos, and become the best driver in Stop The Truckery.
